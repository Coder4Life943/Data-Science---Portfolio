## Module
Project Management

## Result

## Project Overview
This report examines how managing projects supports the successful delivery of business strategy.

The assignment explores how organisations use project management principles to achieve strategic objectives, improve efficiency, and manage organisational change.

The report is based on academic literature and case study examples to analyse how project management contributes to business success.

## Key Topics Covered

- Strategic alignment between projects and organisational goals
- Risk management in projects
- Governance structures in project environments
- Programme and Project Support Office (PPSO)
- Resource allocation
- Stakeholder management
- Benefits realisation
- Project performance measurement
